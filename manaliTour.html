MANALI TOUR

Tour Name: MANALI TOUR EX DELHI

Places Covered: Manali Ex Delhi

Duration: 03 Nights / 4 Days

  • Travel, Lifestyle

Travel Itinerary

Day 01: Delhi to Manali

Day Description: In the morning, meet your driver at Delhi Airport and embark on a scenic journey to Manali (580 Kms/ 11-12 Hrs Approx). Upon arrival in Manali, check into the pre-booked hotel. Enjoy dinner and an overnight stay at the hotel in Manali.

Day 02: Manali - Solang Valley and Local Sightseeing

Day Description: After breakfast, meet your driver and head to Solang Valley. Engage in snow activities such as skiing, skating, horse riding, and snow scooters. Return to Manali and visit Hadimba Temple, Club House, and Van Vihar. Spend the evening exploring Mall Road and the Tibetan Market for shopping. Enjoy dinner and an overnight stay at the hotel in Manali.

Day 03: Manali to Manikaran

Day Description: In the morning, meet your driver and proceed to Manikaran (90 Kms/ 03 Hrs Approx). Explore the famous Sikh Gurudwara, Ram Mandir, and Temple of Shiva. Manikaran is renowned for its hot water springs with temperatures below freezing, allowing you to boil rice and cereals in minutes. Return to Manali, en-route visit Kasol Valley and Kullu Valley. Kasol is known for the picturesque Parvati Valley, while Kullu offers river rafting and shawl factories. Enjoy river rafting at your own risk and expenses. Explore the shawl production houses. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Manali.

Day 04: Departure for Delhi Airport

Day Description: After breakfast, check out from the hotel. Meet your driver and commence the journey to Delhi Airport (590 Kms/ 12-14 Hrs Approx). Bid farewell to the beautiful landscapes and memories of Manali as you head back to Delhi.
