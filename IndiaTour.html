INDIA TOUR

Tour Name: INDIA TOUR

Places Covered: Delhi - Agra – Jaipur – Mandawa – Bikaner – Jaisalmer – Jodhpur – Udaipur - Kochi - Munnar - Thekkady – Alleppey - Mumbai

Duration: 20 Nights / 21 Days

  • Travel, Lifestyle

Day 01: ARRIVE MUMBAI

Our representative will receive you at Mumbai airport, and you will be transferred to the hotel. Overnight stay at the hotel in Mumbai.

Day 02: MUMBAI

After breakfast, proceed towards Elephanta Caves for a sightseeing tour. These caves are located on an island a few miles away from the Gateway of India. Reach the caves by ferry and explore the rock-cut temple dedicated to Lord Shiva. Afternoon cruise back to Gateway of India, followed by visits to Marine Drive, Juhu Beach, and Haji Ali Dargah. Return to the hotel in the evening for an overnight stay.

Day 03: MUMBAI – UDAIPUR (BY FLIGHT)

After breakfast, proceed to Mumbai Airport to board your onward flight to Udaipur. Arrive and check into the hotel. Evening explore the local bazaar to experience the lifestyle of the local people. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 04: UDAIPUR SIGHTSEEING

After breakfast, have a city tour of Udaipur covering Saheliyon Ki Bari, Jagdish Temple, City Palace, and Bhartiya Lok Kala Mandir (Folk Art Museum). Enjoy an evening boat ride at Lake Pichola (subject to water level in the Lake). Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 05: UDAIPUR – JODHPUR

After breakfast, drive to Jodhpur. Arrive and check-in to the hotel. Evening at leisure. Night stay in Jodhpur.

Day 06: JODHPUR SIGHTSEEING

After a relaxed breakfast, proceed for a city sightseeing tour of Jodhpur. Visit the Mehrangarh Fort, Moti Mahal, Phool Mahal, Jaswant Thada, and Umaid Public Gardens. Night stay in Jodhpur.

Day 07: JODHPUR - JAISALMER

After breakfast, drive to Jaisalmer. On arrival, check into the hotel. Evening explore around this magnificent fort city. Night stay in Jaisalmer.

Day 08: JAISALMER

After breakfast, proceed for city sightseeing of Jaisalmer covering Jaisalmer Fort, Sun Gate, Megh Durbar, various temples, Salim Singh-Ki-Haveli, Patwon-Ki-Haveli, and Lodurva. Enjoy a Camel Ride at the sand dunes during sunset. Night stay in Jaisalmer.

Day 09: JAISALMER - BIKANER

After breakfast, drive to Bikaner City. Visit the Junagarh Fort, with palaces like Anup Mahal, Karan Mahal, and Bijai Phool Mahal. Night stay in Bikaner.

Day 10: BIKANER - MANDAWA

After breakfast, drive to Mandawa, known for its painted havelis. Arrive and check into the hotel. Evening visit Shekhawati area and Painted Haveli followed by a one-hour camel ride. Night stay in Mandawa.

Day 11: MANDAWA - JAIPUR

After breakfast, travel to Jaipur, the pink city of Rajasthan. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 12: JAIPUR SIGHTSEEING

After breakfast, proceed for a city sightseeing tour of Jaipur. Visit Amber Fort, City Palace, Jantar Mantar, Hawa Mahal, and Albert Hall. After sightseeing, transfer to the hotel.

Day 13: JAIPUR - FATEHPUR SIKRI - AGRA

After breakfast, travel to Agra. En-route, visit the red sandstone city of Fatehpur Sikri. Upon arrival in Agra, check into the hotel for overnight stay.

Day 14: AGRA SIGHTSEEING – DELHI

After breakfast, visit the Taj Mahal and Agra Fort. Drive back to Delhi. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 15: DELHI SIGHTSEEING

After breakfast, enjoy a sightseeing tour of Delhi, covering Qutab Minar, India Gate, Red Fort, Raj Ghat, Jama Masjid, Chandni Chowk, Laxmi Narayan Temple, and Rashtrapati Bhawan. Spend the remaining part of the day at leisure. Overnight stay at the hotel in Delhi.

Day 16: DELHI – BHUBANESWAR BY FLIGHT

After breakfast, proceed to Delhi Airport to board your onward flight to Bhubaneswar. Arrive and check into the hotel. If time permits, half-day sightseeing of tribal museum (closed on Monday), Khandagiri & Udaigiri Jain caves. Overnight at the hotel.

Day 17: BHUBANESWAR

Morning after breakfast, visit famous city temples including Lingaraj, Parsurameswar, Mukteswar, and Rajarani temple. After lunch, visit Nandankanan Zoo. Overnight at the hotel.

Day 18: BHUBANESWAR – DHAULI – HIRAPUR – PIPLI – RAGHURAJPUR - PURI

Drive to Puri, en-route visit Dhauli (Buddhist stupa), Hirapur (64 yogini temple), Pipli (the appliqué work village), and Raghurajpur (the artisan village). Proceed to Puri and check into the hotel. Evening visit the Lord Jagannath Temple and relax on the golden beach. Overnight at the hotel.

Day 19: PURI – KONARK – PURI

Drive to Konark to visit the Sun Temple. En-route visit Ramchandi temple & Chandrabhaga beach. Return back to Puri. Evening visit the beach market. Overnight at the hotel.

Day 20: PURI – CHILIKA – PURI

Drive to Chilika lake at Satpada. Take a boat cruise to visit the Irrawaddy Dolphins & Rajhans Island. Return back to Puri. Relax on the golden beach. Overnight at the hotel.

Day 21: PURI – BHUBANESWAR BY DRIVE – MUMBAI BY FLIGHT

Drive from Puri to Bhubaneshwar airport to board a flight for Mumbai. Arrive in Mumbai and transfer to the hotel. Overnight at the hotel.
