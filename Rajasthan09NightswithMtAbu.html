Rajasthan 09 Nights with Mt Abu

Tour Name: Rajasthan 09 Nights with Mt Abu

Places Covered: Rajasthan

Duration: 09 Nights / 10 Days

  • Travel, Lifestyle

Day 01: ARRIVE JAIPUR (26/01/2018)

Day Description: Our representative will receive you at Jaipur airport/Railway Station, and you will be transferred to the hotel. Overnight stay at the hotel in Jaipur.

Day 02: JAIPUR SIGHTSEEING (27/01/2018)

Day Description: After breakfast, proceed for a city sightseeing tour of Jaipur:

  • AMBER FORT: Visit the majestic Amber Fort, a former royal capital, reached by riding elephants against the backdrop of wooded hills.
  • CITY PALACE: Explore the City Palace, a delightful blend of Mughal and traditional Rajasthani architecture, housing Chandra Mahal, Shri Govind Dev Temple, and the City Palace Museum.
  • JANTAR MANTAR: Visit the largest and best-preserved observatory built by Jai Singh II, featuring outsized astronomical instruments still in use.
  • HAWA MAHAL: Explore the ornamental facade of the "Palace of Winds," a prominent landmark with five-storey structures adorned with fine trelliswork and balconies.
  • ALBERT HALL: Visit the Central Museum, located at Ram Nivas Garden, offering insights into the culture and traditions of Rajasthan. Learn many unknown and hidden facts about the Rajasthani people. Night stay in Jaipur.

Day 03: JAIPUR - BIKANER (350 Kms / 06 Hrs) (28/01/2018)

Day Description: After breakfast, drive to Bikaner City. Visit the Junagarh Fort, built in 1611 AD, with palaces like Anup Mahal, Karan Mahal, and Bijai Phool Mahal. Night stay in Bikaner.

Day 04: BIKANER – JAISALMER – SAND DUNES (325 Kms / 06 Hrs) (29/01/2018)

Day Description: After breakfast, drive to Jaisalmer. Arrive in Jaisalmer and proceed to Sand Dunes for a camel ride, witnessing the sunset, and arranging a Candlelight Dinner at Sand Dunes. Overnight at Camp.

Day 05: JAISALMER SIGHTSEEING (30/01/2018)

Day Description: After breakfast, proceed for city sightseeing of Jaisalmer covering Jaisalmer Fort, Sun Gate, Megh Durbar, various temples, Salim Singh-Ki-Haveli, Patwon-Ki-Haveli, and Lodurva. Night stay in Jaisalmer.

Day 06: JAISALMER - JODHPUR (290 Kms / 05 Hrs) (31/01/2018)

Day Description: After breakfast, drive to Jodhpur. Visit Meharangarh Fort and Jaswant Thada. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 07: JODHPUR – MOUNT ABU (270 km 6hrs) (01/02/2018)

Day Description: After breakfast, drive to Mount Abu. On arrival, check-in at the hotel. Evening visit sunset point & enjoy a boat ride at Nakki Lake (at your own expense). Nakki Lake, virtually located in the heart of Mount Abu, is a big attraction with tourists. Overnight at Hotel.

Day 08: MOUNT ABU – UDAIPUR (160km 2-3 hrs) (02/02/2018)

Day Description: After breakfast, proceed for half-day Sightseeing, visit the world-famous Dilwara Jain Temple. Dilwara Temple is a group of remarkable Jain temples. After that, proceed to Udaipur and check into the hotel. Overnight at the hotel.

Day 09: UDAIPUR SIGHTSEEING (03/02/2018)

Day Description: After breakfast, have a city tour of Udaipur covering Saheliyon-Ki-Bari, Jagdish Temple, City Palace, and Bhartiya Lok Kala Mandir (Folk Art Museum). Enjoy an evening boat ride at Lake Pichola (subject to water level in the Lake). Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 10: DEPARTURE (04/02/2018)

Day Description: After breakfast, transfer to Uda ipur Airport/Railway Station for your onward journey.
