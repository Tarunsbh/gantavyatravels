BEST OF UTTARANCHAL

Tour Name: BEST OF UTTARANCHAL

Places Covered: Corbett and Nainital Ex Delhi

Duration: 04 Nights / 05 Days

  • Travel, Lifestyle

Travel Itinerary

Day 01: Delhi to Nainital

Day Description: In the morning, your driver will pick you up from Nizamuddin Railway Station and drive you straight to Nainital (300 Kms/ 07-08 Hrs Approx). Upon arrival in Nainital, check into the pre-booked hotel. Overnight stay at the hotel in Nainital.

Nainital: A Himalayan resort town in the Kumaon region of Uttarakhand, Nainital is set around Nainital Lake, a popular boating site with the Naina Devi Hindu Temple on its north shore. Explore the town and enjoy the scenic beauty.

Day 02: Nainital Conference

Day Description: After a leisurely breakfast, attend a full-day meeting at the conference hall (lunch at additional cost). Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 03: Nainital Local Sightseeing

Day Description: After breakfast, meet your driver and spend the day exploring Nainital. Visit places of interest such as the Naina Devi temple, Lands End, Tiffin Top, and the Zoo. Optionally, take the ropeway (on direct payment basis). In the evening, visit Mall Road and Naini Lake for boating (on direct payment basis). Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 04: Nainital to Jim Corbett - Enjoy Wildlife Safari

Day Description: After breakfast, check-out from the hotel and drive to Jim Corbett National Park (70 Kms/ 02 Hrs Approx). On arrival, enjoy a welcome drink at the pre-booked hotel. Later, proceed for an evening safari to experience wildlife. Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 05: Jim Corbett to Delhi

Day Description: Early in the morning, embark on an adventurous Wildlife Jeep Safari as part of your Uttarakhand holiday package. Explore the park, famous for spotting tigers, through the Shambar road. The 3-hour game drive offers glimpses of the park's diverse flora and fauna. Return to the hotel for breakfast, check-out, and drive straight to Delhi Railway Station (280 Kms/ 08-09 Hrs Approx).
