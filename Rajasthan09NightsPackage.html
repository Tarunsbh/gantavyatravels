Rajasthan 09 Nights Package

Tour Name: Rajasthan 09 Nights Package

Places Covered: Rajasthan

Duration: 09 Nights / 10 Days

  • Travel, Lifestyle

Travel Itinerary

Day 01: ARRIVE JAIPUR

Day Description: Our representative will receive you at Jaipur airport/Railway Station, and you will be transferred to the hotel. Overnight stay at the hotel in Delhi.

Day 02: JAIPUR SIGHTSEEING

Day Description: After breakfast, proceed for a city sightseeing tour of Jaipur:

  • AMBER FORT: Visit the majestic Amber Fort, a former royal capital, reached by riding elephants against the backdrop of wooded hills.
  • CITY PALACE: Explore the City Palace, a blend of Mughal and traditional Rajasthani architecture, housing Chandra Mahal, Shri Govind Dev Temple, and the City Palace Museum.
  • JANTAR MANTAR: Visit the largest and best-preserved observatory built by Jai Singh II, featuring outsized astronomical instruments still in use.
  • HAWA MAHAL: Explore the ornamental facade of the "Palace of Winds," a prominent landmark with five-storey structures adorned with fine trelliswork and balconies.
  • ALBERT HALL: Visit the Central Museum, located at Ram Nivas Garden, offering insights into the culture and traditions of Rajasthan.

Night stay in Jaipur.

Day 03: JAIPUR - BIKANER (350 Kms / 06 Hrs)

Day Description: After breakfast, drive to Bikaner City. Visit the Junagarh Fort, built in 1611 AD, with palaces like Anup Mahal, Karan Mahal, and Bijai Phool Mahal. Night stay in Bikaner.

Day 04: BIKANER – JAISALMER – SAND DUNES (325 Kms / 06 Hrs)

Day Description: After breakfast, drive to Jaisalmer. Arrive in Jaisalmer and proceed to Sand Dunes for a camel ride and witnessing the sunset. Enjoy a Candlelight Dinner at Sand Dunes. Overnight at Camp.

Day 05: JAISALMER SIGHTSEEING

Day Description: After breakfast, proceed for city sightseeing of Jaisalmer covering Jaisalmer Fort, Sun Gate, Megh Durbar, Salim Singh-Ki-Haveli, PatwonKi-Haveli, and Lodurva. Night stay in Jaisalmer.

Day 06: JAISALMER - JODHPUR (290 Kms / 05 Hrs)

Day Description: After breakfast, drive to Jodhpur. The remaining day is free for leisure. Overnight at the hotel.

Day 07: JODHPUR SIGHTSEEING

Day Description: After a relaxed breakfast, proceed for a city sightseeing tour of Jodhpur. Visit Mehrangarh Fort, Moti Mahal, Phool Mahal, Jaswant Thada, and Umaid Public Gardens. Night stay in Jodhpur.

Day 08: JODHPUR - UDAIPUR VIA RANAKPUR (275 Kms / 06 Hrs)

Day Description: Morning drive to Udaipur. En-route visit Ranakpur temples, the pilgrimage center of Rajasthan. Arrive and check into the hotel. Evening walk around the local bazaar. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 09: UDAIPUR SIGHTSEEING

Day Description: After breakfast, have a city tour of Udaipur covering SaheliyonKiBari, Jagdish Temple, City Palace, and Bhartiya Lok Kala Mandir (Folk Art Museum). Enjoy an evening boat ride at Lake Pichola (subject to water level in the Lake). Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 10: DEPARTURE

Day Description: After breakfast, transfer to Udaipur Airport/Railway Station for your onward journey.
