KASHMIR TOUR PACKAGE

Tour Name: KASHMIR TOUR PACKAGE

Places Covered: Kashmir

Duration: 09 Nights / 10 Days

  • Travel, Lifestyle

Travel Itinerary

Day 01: Arrive Srinagar (Overnight Stay at Houseboat in Dal Lake)

Day Description: On arrival at the Srinagar airport and transfer to Houseboat. In evening Shikara ride in world famous Dal Lake. Later return houseboat and overnight stay at the houseboat.

Day 02: Srinagar – Local Sightseeing (Overnight Stay at Hotel in Srinagar)

Day Description: Today we will do full day of local sightseeing that includes a visit to the famous Mughal gardens of Nishat and Shalimar. Built in between a period of 14 years the two gardens are a heritage left behind by the erstwhile Mughal, whose taste for laying exquisite gardens was fascinating. With a backdrop of the sprawling Zabarwan Mountains, the gardens boast of green turfs, blooming flowers and cascading water fountains. We will explore the gardens and later drive back to the hotel for a dinner and overnight stay.

Day 03: Srinagar – Gulmarg (Overnight Stay at Hotel in Gulmarg)

Day Description: After breakfast in the morning, proceed towards Gulmarg. Enroute you get to see the beautiful Tangmarg town and drive ahead on a scenic drive of 14 kilometers to Gulmarg. Arrive in Gulmarg early in the afternoon and check in at the hotel. Later, begin a short tour, boarding the Gondola cable car system (the 08 minutes ropeway). Descend back to Gulmarg after an hour and later indulge in some horse-riding. Stay overnight at hotel in Gulmarg.

Day 04: at Gulmarg (Overnight Stay at Hotel in Gulmarg)

Day Description: After breakfast, day leisure or do some sightseeing in Gulmarg. Overnight stay at hotel.

Day 05: Gulmarg – Pahalgam (Overnight Stay at Hotel in Pahalgam)

Day Description: After Breakfast, we drive to Pahalgam via Pampore, Avantipura and the village of Bijbehara which remains famous as the bread basket of Kashmir. We switch from the national highway 1A at Khanabal and drive through the second largest city of Anantnag. From here the road turns scenic as we drive parallel on the Lidder River flowing from the opposite direction. In Pahalgam, check-in at the hotel and spend the rest of the day at leisure. Overnight stay at the hotel in Pahalgam.

Day 06: at Pahalgam (Overnight Stay at Hotel in Pahalgam)

Day Description: After breakfast, day leisure or do some sightseeing in Pahalgam. Overnight stay at hotel.

Day 07: Pahalgam – Srinagar (Overnight Stay at Hotel in Srinagar)

Day Description: After breakfast, enjoy a leisurely day and later proceed to Srinagar. Check into the hotel and refresh. In the afternoon, take a walk along Boulevard Road. In the evening, return to Srinagar for an overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 08: Srinagar – Sonamarg – Srinagar (Overnight Stay at Hotel in Srinagar)

Day Description: After breakfast, embark on a full-day excursion to Sonamarg, known as the Meadows of Gold and one of the most beautiful drives from Srinagar. Located at a height of 2692 meters, you have the option to take a pony ride (at your own cost) to Thajiwas Glacier, where snow remains throughout the year. Overnight stay at the Srinagar hotel.

Day 09: Srinagar – Yousmarg – Srinagar (Overnight Stay at Hotel in Srinagar)

Day Description: After a hearty breakfast, embark on an exciting two-hour journey to Yousmarg, a relatively unknown destination. The drive takes you across grassy meadows, surrounded by pine forests and majestic snow-clad mountains. Explore the rolling meadows and stroll along flower-strewn meadows towards the Doodh Ganga. Visit a historical and religious site with the tomb of Sheikh Nu-Ud-Din. Return to Srinagar in the evening and transfer to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 10: Srinagar Departure

Day Description: Early in the morning, after breakfast, transfer to your waiting vehicle for the journey to the airport for your flight home.
